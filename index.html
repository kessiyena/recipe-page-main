Simple omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, herbs, or cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper...
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat...
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling...
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges...
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings...
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
